Using a timeless century old recipe closely guarded by the You Me & Momos family for generations, You Me & Momos’ ready-to-eat Momos are an improved modern reinterpretation of the Original Himalayan Momos. Made with premium ingredients and no preservatives, and crafted Himalayan-style with thin wrappers that allow for the perfect balance of flavours, these bite-sized treats are full of flavour and easy to prepare. Today, You Me & Momos is bringing this timeless family recipe and blending it with Japanese innovation to revive the original taste of Momos.
